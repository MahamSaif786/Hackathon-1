# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics – Introduction

Let’s explore **Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics in a clear and practical way**.

## Getting Started

This book is designed to help you **understand how intelligent machines interact with the physical world**.

You’ll learn how **AI, sensors, actuators, and robotics** come together to build **humanoid and autonomous systems**.

## What you’ll learn

By the end of this book, you will understand:

- Fundamentals of **Physical AI**
- Core concepts of **Humanoid Robotics**
- How robots **perceive, think, and act**
- Real-world applications of humanoid robots
- Ethical and future considerations of intelligent machines

## Who this book is for

This book is suitable for:

- Students of **Computer Science, AI, and Robotics**
- Beginners interested in **humanoid robots**
- Developers transitioning from **software AI to physical systems**
- Anyone curious about the **future of intelligent machines**

## How to use this book

You can read this book:

- **Chapter by chapter** for structured learning
- Or jump directly to topics like **robot motion, perception, or control**

Each section is written in **simple language**, supported with **examples and explanations** to make complex ideas easy to understand.
